Kent County Hog Roast

Enquiries FAQs Hogroast Packages
Kent County Hog Roast is a family run business based in Borough Green in Kent. We specialise in providing everything you need for a high quality hog roast or BBQ catering in Kent and the surrounding areas whether it is a Wedding, private party or corporate event. Our aim is to provide the highest quality experience and ensure that we provide you with everything you'll need to host a great hog roast at your event, from the roasting machine and the cooking gas, to the succulent hog itself. We have trained chefs on hand to prepare, cook and serve up your hog roast to your guests, meaning you won't need to worry about a thing on the day. We are flexible in our approach, if you have an enquiry outside of what is listed on the Website, please get in touch and we will do our best to meet your requirements. Remember that it does not need to be a "Hog" Roast - You may prefer something a little different, Beef, Lamb, Wild Boar, or Venison for example.

High Quality Produce

Kent County Hog Roast use locally sourced produce and are in partnership with P W Copus Butchers to ensure that we provide you with the highest quality meats. P W Copus Butchers are based at 72 Western Road, Borough Green, Kent, TN15 8AH. You are more than welcome to pop into the shop at any time to enquire about the meats and services we provide and you will also have the option to hand select the type of meats you would like at your event if you are hosting a tasty BBQ / Hog Roast.

Excellent Service

Our team pride ourselves on delivering a high quality, personable and effective experience. From the moment, you contact us, one of our team will provide you with all of the options available and will be able to provide you with advice when arranging catering for your event in terms of the quantities and quality of meats available. We will keep in regular contact with you right up to the day of your event, and if required can liaise with your venue or other event companies to ensure that we work in partnership and your day runs smoothly. Our set up team will be with you up to seven hours before you would like the Hog Roast calving so that we can ensure that the Hog cooks succulently and gives you that crunchy crackling everybody loves. Our team will be we well presented. Our chefs will be in traditional chef whites and our team in uniform. We want to give you a professional and friendly service on your day. After the event, we will ensure that everything is tidied away. We would love to hear your experiences following the event and please feel free to share these with us, whether its pictures or comments.
