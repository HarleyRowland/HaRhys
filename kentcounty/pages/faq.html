FAQs

1. How many people will a Hog Roast cater for?

A full hog will cater for up to 200 guests. A half hog will feed up to 100 guests.

2. How long before the event will you need to set up?

Our team will be with you up to seven hours before your event, this is so that we have time to set up and can get the Hog cooking.

3. How long does a full pig take to cook?

We use high quality machinery and a full hog usually takes between 5-6 hours cooking time.

4. How much will it cost?

Please visit our Hog Roast packages page to give you an idea on costs. If you just require the equipment, machine hire, gas, set up, delivery and collection is only Â£150.

If our packages do not meet your requirements, please contact us and we can pull together a bespoke package.

5. Are you registered?

Yes, we are registered with local authority environmental officers and have HACCP in place.

6. Are you insured?

Yes, we hold public liability insurance which allows us to operate almost anywhere. We can provide relevant documents upon request to venues etc.

7. Where is your meat from?

We use locally sourced produce and are in partnership with P W Copus Butchers to ensure that we provide you with the highest quality meats.

8. What do I need to provide?

All we need you to do is have a flat area 3m x3m square for us to set up on put up our Gazebo up which goes over the entire carvery which means come rain or shine we can still operate efficiently. Other than that we are self-contained and you can sit back and enjoy the day.

9. How far do you travel?

Kent County Hog Roast operate all over Kent and the surrounding areas.

10. How will your team look on the day?

Our team will be we well presented. Our chefs will be in traditional chef whites and our team in uniform. We want to give you a professional and friendly service on your day.

11. What happens after the event?

After the event, we will ensure that everything is tidied away. We would love to hear your experiences following the event and please feel free to share these with us, whether it's pictures or comments.
